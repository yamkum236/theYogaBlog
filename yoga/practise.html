This page is dedicated to learning all about Yoga, what exactly is it and why you should being doing it and implementing it in your life.

Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India and aim to control and still the mind, recognizing a detached witness-consciousness untouched by the mind and mundane suffering.

    The word "yoga" comes from the root word "yuj" which essentially means "to yoke" or "to bind".
    The word itself has an underlying theme of being connected. Various styles of yoga combine movement, 
    physical postures, meditation and breathing techniques or relaxation to promote mental and physical well-being.
